Hen/Birthday and Team Building Parties

We offer hen parties in burlesque and/ or pole dancing. We can either come to you at a suitable venue or we can host it at our studio. We can supply a buff butler to add to the fun on the day. We finish the parties off with some fun games to get you in the mood.

All parties are suitable for any level of fitness and any experience. We tailor them to the needs of the individuals on the day.

We have a small coffee shop that can be opened on the day where you can relax before the party.

For more information or to book please email us or call 01383 626006.
