About us

At Sunshine Fitness we offer fitness classes, personal training, one to ones and pride ourselves on bringing you the highest quality pole dancing classes from the beautiful Sunshine Pole Studio. We run an all-inclusive, safe environment, with a "come as you are" ethos!

Our ethos

Come as you are

This means you do not need to shave, lose weight, gain fitness or strength or change in any way to attend our classes. Sunshine is for absolutely anyone and we are a safe and inclusive environment for you to be 100% your authentic self.
